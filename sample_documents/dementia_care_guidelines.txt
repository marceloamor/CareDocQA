Dementia Care Best Practices

Communication:
- Use simple, reassuring language
- Maintain eye contact and a calm tone
- Avoid correcting or arguing over memory lapses

Environment:
- Label rooms clearly (e.g., “Toilet”, “Dining Room”)
- Reduce noise and clutter to prevent agitation

Daily Support:
- Maintain a consistent routine
- Encourage light exercise and social activity
- Record any significant changes in mood or behaviour
