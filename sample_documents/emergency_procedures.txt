Emergency Procedures for Residential Units

1. Fire Evacuation
   - Raise alarm immediately
   - Assist residents to exit via nearest safe route
   - Assemble at the car park
   - Contact fire services (999)

2. Medical Emergency
   - Check for responsiveness and breathing
   - Call emergency services
   - Notify duty nurse or lead carer
   - Record incident details in the care log

3. Missing Resident
   - Search immediate surroundings
   - Alert management and local authorities
   - Document time, description, and actions taken
